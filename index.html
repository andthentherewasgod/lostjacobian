The Lost Jacobian - ZIMBABWE

The International Seabed Authority is on the precipice of allowing contractors to begin mining the deep sea before the scale of the environmental consequences have been assessed, informed regulation is drawn, and necessary protections have been put in place.
My goal is to broadcast this state of play and help mobilise a global push for a moritorium on deep sea mining.

NON-GOVERNMENTAL ORGANISATION (COMING SOON)

The Ocean Rights of Landlocked Countries - ZIMBABWE

Under UNCLOS, the Deep Sea is the common heritage of all humankind, however the interests of developing countries, especially landlocked developing nations are largely being overlooked under the current ISA structures.
We seek to change this through a collaboration of landlocked countries.

MARINE BIO-CHEM START-UP

Phytolutions - GERMANY

At Phytolutions we were on the frontier of creating algae based solutions in sustainable development, including algal bio-fuels and algae-based waste-water treatments.
